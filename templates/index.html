sock cam

What is Sock?

Sock is a common variety goldfish. He was a prize at an event in June 2009, so he is at least 11 and a half years old as of the beginning of 2021. He is between 10-12 inches long. He started out at about an inch long!

Why are there two fish?

Glad you asked! Sock recently got a brother from our local fish store. He is also a common variety goldfish, aptly named Shoe. We are guessing he is 3-5 years old based on his size.

Why doesn't Shoe come closer to the camera?

Shoe is a bully so he is in fish jail on the other side of the tank.

Did you just assume their genders?!?!?

Both fish have breeding spots indicating that they are males. So, no.

Why is the video feed so choppy?

This is running on a Raspberry Pi Model B from like 2012. It's not a very powerful server.

Why is the video feed dark/black?

It is nighttime. Fish need to sleep too!
